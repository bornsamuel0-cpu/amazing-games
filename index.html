About Us

Amazing Games is an independent Roblox game studio founded in January 2025 with one clear mission: to invest in, build, and publish amazing games with real front-page potential.

We focus on creating experiences that are not only fun to play, but also designed to succeed long-term through strong retention, smart monetization, and replayable core gameplay loops.

Our studio believes great Roblox games come from the balance of creativity and strategy. This means thoughtful game design, clean and optimized scripting, strong visual identity, polished UI, and monetization that enhances gameplay rather than hurting it.

Since our founding, Amazing Games has operated with a long-term mindset: build quality first, learn from every release, and continuously raise our standards.
